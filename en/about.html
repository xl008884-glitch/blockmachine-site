About BLOCKMASTERY

20+ years building block making machines for African and global construction markets. Direct from our Chinese factory.

Who We Are

We are a leading China-based OEM manufacturer with more than 20 years of experience designing and building block making machines for the global construction market. Our product line covers the QT4-18 fully automatic hydraulic production line, the QT4-40 and QT4-40A semi-automatic block machines, hollow block machines, non-fired brick machines, and concrete mixers — everything you need to start or scale a brick factory.

Our machines are trusted by customers in 80+ countries across Asia, Africa, the Americas, Europe and Oceania. We hold ISO 9001:2015 quality management certification and full CE marking — accepted at African customs without delay. Annual export volume reaches millions of US dollars, with the fastest growth coming from Sub-Saharan Africa.

Why Customers Choose Us

  • Direct factory pricing, no middlemen — buy at manufacturer cost and reinvest the margin in your business.
  • Custom configuration available — moulds, vibration motors, hydraulic systems and control panels tailored to your local market.
  • 1-year warranty on main components, plus lifetime technical support via WhatsApp in English and French.
  • Spare parts in stock — vibration motors, hydraulic seals, mould plates and electronics ship within 48 hours from our warehouse.
  • On-site installation guidance for bulk QT4-18 orders — our engineer travels to your country to commission the line.
