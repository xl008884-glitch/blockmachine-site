QT4-18 Fully Automatic Hydraulic Production Line

Our flagship model. The QT4-18 is a fully automatic hydraulic press with PLC control — operator presses one button, the machine feeds material, presses, demoulds, and stacks on its own. The high-pressure hydraulic system produces interlocking pavers and hourdis blocks that meet European and South African SABS standards.

Why the QT4-18 Leads the Range

  • High Level of Automation — automatic feeding + automatic pallet distribution significantly reduces manual labour
  • Advanced PLC Control — integrated PLC control system enables seamless full automation and efficient production cycles
  • Superior Compression & Strength — vertical directional compression vibration produces blocks that can be stacked 3-5 layers high
  • Unmatched Versatility — change moulds to produce hollow, solid, hourdis, and interlocking paver blocks
