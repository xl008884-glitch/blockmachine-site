Why the QT4-40 Works

The QTJ4-40 is one of our best-selling models, designed for first-time brick factory owners. It runs on a heavy-duty 350-type reducer, and all rotating parts have been upgraded to bearing-grade components. The frame is built from 5 mm thick national-standard square steel with a wear-resistant inner liner. Four guide columns lock the mould in exact position, extending mould life and keeping block dimensions consistent.

Two 2.2 kW lower vibrators work with a 3 kW upper vibrator to deliver 8 – 10 MPa compaction pressure on hollow blocks and 10 – 12 MPa on solid bricks. By changing the mould, the same machine produces hollow blocks, solid bricks, pavers, grass-planting bricks, slope-protection bricks, kerb stones and floor slabs.
